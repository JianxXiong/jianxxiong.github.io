About Me

I am a graduate student in School of Computer Science at Fudan University, supervised by Prof. Dejing Dou. I am also a member of Fudan DIL Lab. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng degree from College of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Hohai University in Jun. 2024 and I was admitted to Fudan University without entrance examination.

My research interests include: Reinforcement Learning, LLM Agent and MLLM.
